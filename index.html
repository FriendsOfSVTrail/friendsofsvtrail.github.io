Welcome

The Friends of Snoqualmie Valley Trail and River are a group of citizens committed to safeguarding and preserving the natural beauty and open space that embody the rural character of North Bend. Residents and visitors are drawn to North Bend because of its awe-inspiring beauty and prevalence of vegetation, wildlife, and outdoor opportunities. The quality of life for people who live and visit North Bend is “directly associated with the quality of the environment. Protection of these elements is essential if residents are to maintain a healthy lifestyle,” per the North Bend Comprehensive Plan. As members of the Friends of Snoqualmie Valley Trail and River we believe that protection of our dense forests, fish and wildlife, water for drinking & rivers, and open spaces are a fundamental right. As stated in the North Bend Comprehensive Plan, “The contamination or reduction of these resources where people reside and work, damages the very reasons that people choose to live here.” We have organized ourselves around this goal. If you are of a like mind and wish to help shape North Bend within this vision, you are welcome to join this group.
